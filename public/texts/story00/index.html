Mam conversation 1

by Madame Rosalie N'Diadan

View options

hɛ yí khúúnbrɛn ɛ tɛ́ á yɔn

Everything you do is good,

á yɔn á yɔn

is good, is good.

thábɛn lephan nɔ̂n a bha gɛ yɛ́n hɛ hɛ́nphe ɛ bhú

Who can know what you think?

thábɛn nɔ̂n a bha gɛ wú hɛ yí ɛ wú

Who can see what you see?

hɛ nɔ̂n ɛ li áyí khúúnbrɛn óthe

It's you who is the master of everything.

o tɛ bhrɔ́bhrɔ́ bɛn ló khɛ́n brɛ tɛ nɔ̂n a san

There are a lot of people but he chooses one.

ɔ́n ti bha hɛ́nmɛn ɔ́n ne só khú

Approach, do not be afraid !

yankan mɔ́n trɛ hɔ́n tho

The hand of God is good.
