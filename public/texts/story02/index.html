hébreu nmyɔ́ khwɛn fɔ́n je tho

The children of the Hebrews have brought the branches.

wo elú khwɛ́ ozana

They shout out 'Hosanna.'

davíde nmi ebhá

David's child is coming.

mɛ́n nɔ̂n ń chre frímú nmi tho

It's him who is sitting on the donkey.
